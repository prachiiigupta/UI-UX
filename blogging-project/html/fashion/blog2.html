7 Clothing items to break up with NOW

By Divija Gupta

Whether you are making room for new stuff or are just on a cleaning spree,your wardrobe has always got some clothing stuff which demands retirement.

But where to start from?Which cloth should be ditched first?Lets have a look below to get you some things to part ways with :-

  1. The old and well worn coat

    You love your coat so much so that it has started to break down into pieces.Sewing the buttons or fixing holes might make you happy but this stage has taken up your time and is of no worth.
    Its better to invest in a warmer and sturdy coat before winters kick start.

  2. Faded pair of PJs aka pyjamas

    Who doesn’t love to slouch around in PJs but you never know when you’ll need to rush out in them.To avoid any embarassing situation,its better to go for something fresh, bright, and new.

  3. Blister giving shoes and sandals

    Its does happen that we tend to buy those over-the-top sandals but end up with blisters and pain.
    Well,it might have got you huge bucks to invest but its time you throw them away to avoid anything more.

  4. White sneakers that are adamant to get cleaned

    Even detergent, bleach, and a day out in the sun cannot save your once-white sneakers. If stains really refuse to budge? Just invest in a brand-new pair.

  5. Accidently broken bag

    One fine day you overstuffed your bag and boom-it broke up.
    If you paid a hefty penny for it,head to mend it.But if it’s just something simple you happened to love? Time to let go.

  6. Ill-fitted shirt/dress/pants

    Many of us end up buying ill-fitted stuff and the day never comes up to fix them by a taylor.It keeps sitting in the closet and hasn’t been worn. Rather than letting it waste away in the cupboard, why not donate it or sell it?

  7. Ill-fitted bras

    After a bunch of wears, bras tend to stretch out and lose their shape, which could affect how clothes fit and fall. Invest in new undergarments and remember this primary rule: never put them in the dryer.

Now,you must have got ideas as to what clothes need to be ditched and from where to start!Have a nice day with your wardrobe.

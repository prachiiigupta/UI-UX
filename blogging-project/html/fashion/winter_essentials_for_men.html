6 Essentials Every Guy Must Have for Winters

By Divija Gupta

It is that time of the year when days will be short and sun won’t be lenient to us, yes we are talking about winters.

It is also that time when you get to flaunt your best of the jackets, pullovers, boots et al. If you haven’t started your winter shopping yet and are in a bid either to buy something new or replace some old stuff, then STOP! We have some really cool yet essential stuffs’ list ready for you to surely look out for these winters.

  1. Leather Jacket

    Keep winters at bay with a cosy yet stylish leather jacket in black or brown, which will complement your every outfit. As you’ll wish that it goes on for atleast two seasons, so invest in a good quality jacket.

  2. Pair of sexy boots

    If you don’t want to go for the age old pair of shoes to keep your feet away from snow, then a pair of sexy boots has to there to rescue you. Invest in a good pair with sturdy built and tight grip with a good quality soul. You can go for brown, black or any colour boots as per your choice, either knee length or mid-length.

  3. Warm scarf

    A warm scarf with some bright colour would take your overall winter look a notch higher. Ruffled scarves are also available in market. Go for bright colour scarves in red, maroon, black, blue colours and pair them in contrast to your overall look for some freshness.

  4. Quality winter or over coat

    If you live in some really cold places, then this must be your for sure pick. This would cover you from top to bottom, leaving you hot from inside. If you are investing huge bucks in it, do ensure that it is made of a durable material, is versatile and also lasts for a few winters.



  5. Bulky cardigan or pullover

    Instead of jacket or fleece, invest in a heavy knit cardigan or pullover to wear. Look for something in check pattern with no zippers. Varied colours are available to choose from. Choose one as per your complexion and also versatility.



  6. A cozy tracksuit

    This might not be your pick for any season actually but investing in one is the thing of the wise. It has been one of the fashion trends to follow for winter/autumn 2017-2018 and has also made its way to the ramp shows.

So, these were some of the winter essentials for men. Rock these winters in style and kick out cold with fashion.
